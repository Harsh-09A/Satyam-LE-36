Welcome to Satyam LE 36

Welcome to Le 36 Kharghar, a visionary residential landmark by Satyam Developers, poised to redefine the skyline of Navi Mumbai. Nestled in the strategic locale of Sector 36, Kharghar, this project is not just a home, but an address that signifies a ‘Next Level’ lifestyle. Offering a perfect blend of magnificent residences, world-class amenities, and unparalleled convenience, Le 36 is crafted for those who aspire for more.

This is where ambition meets architecture. Satyam Developers, a powerhouse in real estate, brings together experience and youthful passion to create a masterpiece. Driven by the expertise of minds like Mr. Hitesh Jain and Mr. Karan Gulati, Le 36 is the first of many upcoming landmarks, designed to offer a larger-than-life experience. It’s a promise of a future where every day feels like an upgrade.
